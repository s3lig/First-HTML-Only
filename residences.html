Residences

Our residences are located in the following areas:

  • Ngara: Located in the city center, this residence is ideal for students from:the University of Nairobi, Kenyatta University, and other institutions located in the city center.

  • Parklands: Located in the Westlands area, this residence is ideal for students from the University of Nairobi, Kenyatta University, and other institutions located in the Westlands area.

  • Rongai: Located in the Rongai area, this residence is ideal for students from the Multimedia University, Nazarene University, and other institutions located in the Rongai area.

  • Kasarani: Located in the Kasarani area, this residence is ideal for students from the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, Kenyatta University, and other institutions located in the Kasarani area.

  • Thika: Located in the Thika area, this residence is ideal for students from the Mount Kenya University, Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, and other institutions located in the Thika area.

  • Kitengela: Located in the Kitengela area, this residence is ideal for students from the Multimedia University, Nazarene University, and other institutions located in the Kitengela area.

  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Laundry facilities
  • 24-hour security

Each of our residences has the following amenities:

  • Common areas for socializing and studying

  • Study rooms for quiet and focused work

  • Kitchen facilities for cooking and dining

  • Outdoor spaces for relaxing and enjoying the fresh air
