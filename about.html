About Us

Vijiji Hostels

Vijiji Hostels is a home to a vibrant community of more than 700
residence from various Universities and colloges such as
Strathmore University, Multimedia University, and Kenyatta
University. We offer a variety of rooms to suit your needs,
including single, double, and triple rooms. Our hostel is
located in the heart of Nairobi, close to the city center and
public transportation. We are committed to providing a safe,
comfortable, and welcoming environment for all of our residents.
Whether you are a student, a professional, or a traveler, we
invite you to make Vijiji Hostel your home away from home.
